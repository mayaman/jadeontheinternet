Jade on the Internet (@jadeontheinternet) is an ongoing project and TikTok-based performance by Maya Man focused on the cultivation, presentation, and monetization of femme identity on the Internet. Jade exists on screen and on screen only. Jade reflects what she sees rewarded in the online space she inhabits. Jade does not represent any one IRL person, but instead acts as a generalization of popular existing online performances of feminine identity. Jade has internalized that in her world, bigger numbers are better. Jade is exactly who you think she is... who do you think she is? Send an email to respond... Jade and this website are perpetually under construction, constantly evolving to keep up with perceived demands.
